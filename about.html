私たちの仕事の４つの要素

COMU LLC was established in 2016, to work on projects for community-based spatial transformation. Our projects and activities aim to empower the community so that community themselves can decide how their surrounding spaces can be used and transformed. We work collaboratively with community organisations, makers, craftspeople, and local government, in order to realise a unique space that expresses the livelihood of the people. Some of our expertise include post-disaster development, rural development and international collaboration at local scale.
